# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Our pledge
We pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of background or identity, and to act in ways that foster an open,
welcoming, and healthy community.

## Standards
Examples of positive behaviour: empathy, respect for differing opinions,
graceful acceptance of constructive feedback. Unacceptable behaviour includes
harassment, insulting or derogatory comments, and publishing others' private
information.

## Enforcement
<!-- Replace with a dedicated conduct@ alias once one is available. -->
Report violations privately to the maintainer via GitHub
([@schwepps](https://github.com/schwepps)). Maintainers will review and respond, and may
remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

The full text and enforcement guidelines are available at the link above.
